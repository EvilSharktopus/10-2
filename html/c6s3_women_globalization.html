✓ Ways globalization has helped women

Export manufacturing created millions of paid jobs for women in Bangladesh, Vietnam, Cambodia who had few options before
Microfinance gave women access to capital they were denied by traditional banks
Global awareness campaigns (MeToo, gender pay reporting laws) spread across borders
Women's labour force participation has risen in most developing regions

✗ Ways globalization has harmed women

Garment factories are 80% female — and pay the lowest wages in global supply chains
The gender wage gap persists in every country — women earn 83¢ per $1 at current global pace, won't reach parity for 134 years
Microfinance interest rates as high as 100%+ have trapped some women in debt
1 billion women still excluded from the formal financial system entirely
